sugarland

Sugarland is a website I created to highlight the storyboards for three escape room ideas, as part of a project for a product design class. While the assignment was simply to post our three storyboards onto a website, I wanted to work on my web development skills and experimented a bit with the design. The theme is fun and whimsical, as expected of a land full of giant candy and sweets.
